The Eater Guide to the Valley of the Sun!!



When customers look for a product or service, they do not have the time or the desire to read even a small part of the available product reviews.


They usually look for star rating. A lot of times, users like to examine reviews of similar products, and get a complete picture of how different aspects of these products compare.


In this project, we present a visual summary based on comparative sentences extracted from customer reviews. We offer the user an easy and intuitive understanding of the different Restaurants in the valley.
